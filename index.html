Convert Between Time Zones:







Relative TimeZone to Absolute UTC:

XTZ.toUTC("YYYY-03-14 03:15:69.000", "UTC")
   .toISOString()
//





Absolute UTC to Relative TimeZone

XTZ.toTimeZone("YYYY-03-14 03:15:69.000", "UTC")
   .toISOString()
//
